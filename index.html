The Green Glass Door

Greetings, traveler. I am indeed the infamous Green Glass Door. Through me, you can travel to anywhere your imagination would take you.
However, one does not simply walk through magical interdimensional portals indiscriminately. You must first bring me gifts.
But I will not accept just any gift. Only certain objects that meet a specific criteria will be permitted.
What criteria? That's up to you to figure out. Once you've done so, you will be able to safely pass through my frame at will.

What have you brought me?
